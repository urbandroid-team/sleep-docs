We offer two intertwined apps - Sleep as Android and SleepCloud. Both have free and premium versions.

== FREE Sleep as Android

Free version of the app is available on the https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.urbandroid.sleep[Play Store], or on https://sleep.urbandroid.org/more/download/[our website].

With the free version of Sleep as Android, you get a *4-day trial of the fully functional application* to test all its features and explore its possibilities.

- After the trial expires, you *can still use the app for free*, but sleep tracking is limited to every other day (tracking is limited for tracking started on Tuesday and Thursday).
- Access to graph edits and chart section is limited.
- All non-tracking features will work without limits, including alarms, lullabies, CAPTCHAs, bedtime...
- The free app may display ads after the trial period.

== FULL Sleep as Android

The full version unlocks all Sleep features:

- Sleep tracking without limits
- Smart wakeup without limits
- Long-term charts and advise
- Sleep Assistant
- 64 extra lullabies with https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.urbandroid.sleep.lullaby[extension pack]

There are two options to get full Sleep as Android:

1. In-app Sleep Lifetime
2. Subscription for Sleep

=== In-app Sleep as Android Lifetime

A full app purchased on the Play Store through a link from the app.

- Purchase recognition works automatically.
- If you use multiple Google Accounts, make sure that the account you used to purchase the app is always the primary account in the Play Store app, otherwise Sleep as Android may start acting as a free version (see <</faq/purchase_not_unlocked#, troubleshooting guide>>).


=== Subscription for Sleep as Android

Subscription is mainly for users, who are not yet decided if they would benefit from the lifetime purchase.

- The fee is either monthly, or yearly (depending on your choice).
- Both subscription types includes SleepCloud PRO version.
- Yearly subscription has additional extra week of free trial period.

== FREE SleepCloud

SleepCloud is native backup service for Sleep as Android app, available on the https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.urbandroid.sleep.addon.port[Play Store].

- Automatic updates into the SleepCloud account once a week.
- The https://sleep-cloud.appspot.com/SleepCloud.html[online account] is available without any limits.
- The syncs to the Google Drive and Dropbox are not limited.

== FULL SleepCloud

- Unlimited automatic backups (each time you end the tracking and the phone has connectivity).
- Full SleepCloud version is already included in the monthly subscription plan.
